# Backlink strategy

Target: 10–20 high-quality referring domains within 90 days of launch.

## Priority tactics (in order)

### 1. Spanish podcasting directories and blogs

- **iVoox Blog** — Spain's largest podcast platform publishes editorials. Pitch a guest post: "Los mejores estudios para grabar tu podcast en Valencia".
- **Podcasteros.es** — Spanish podcast community; they accept studio listings.
- **Spreaker Blog (Spanish section)** — educational content, accepts pitches.

### 2. Local Valencia media

- **El Diario del Puerto** (eldiariodelpuerto.com) — local digital. Pitch: "Valencia tiene estudios para grabar podcasts de nivel profesional".
- **Valencia Plaza** (valenciaplaza.com) — business/culture. Pitch: "La economía del podcast: los estudios de Valencia".
- **Valencia Extra** — curated local content, accepts press releases.

### 3. HARO / Qwoted pitches (English)

Query categories to monitor: journalism, tech, media production.
Offer the angle: "Expert on the Valencia podcast scene / best local studio options for creators."

### 4. Valencia startup / entrepreneur directories

- **StartupValencia** — includes resources for content creators.
- **Lanzadera alumni network** — JMartell's studio incubator; editorial reference.
- **Valencia Business Lab** — often curates local service resources.

### 5. Citation sites (NAP consistency + directory links)

- **Páginas Amarillas** — add listing pointing to valenciapodcaststudio.com as editorial.
- **11870.com** — Spanish local directory.
- **Yelp España** — add editorial listing.
- **Hotfrog España** — free business directory.

### 6. Internal link structure (already in the build)

- Every studio page → barrio taxonomy page.
- Every barrio page → homepage + estudios list.
- Every guide → top 3 studios.
- Comparativa → all studios.

## Outreach cadence

- Weeks 1–4: submit to 5 citation sites (low-effort, high NAP consistency value).
- Weeks 2–8: write 2 guest posts (Spanish podcasting community + local Valencia media).
- Weeks 4–12: HARO/Qwoted monitoring, respond to 1–2 relevant queries per week.
- Ongoing: monthly review; update outreach tracker in this doc.

## Link quality criteria

Accept: DA ≥ 20, Spanish-language or local Valencia focus, no link farms.
Reject: paid link networks, foreign-language-only sites, high-spam-score domains.
